About Animal Aid SA

Founded in 2010 by veterinarians and animal advocates in Johannesburg, Animal Aid SA is dedicated to saving, healing, and rehoming abused and abandoned animals. With over 4,500 rescues and partnerships with 20 shelters, our mission grows stronger each year.

Our Mission & Vision

To ensure the humane treatment of animals through rescue, rehabilitation, adoption, and public education—building a compassionate culture in South Africa.

A nation where every animal lives free from cruelty and neglect, nurtured by a caring and educated society.
