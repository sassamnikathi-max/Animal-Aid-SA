About Animal Aid SA

Based in Johannesburg, we rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals while educating communities.

Mission

Ensure humane treatment of animals through rescue operations, veterinary care, rehabilitation, adoption services, and education.

Vision

A compassionate South Africa where every animal is treated with kindness and respect.

Core Programs

  • Emergency rescue operations
  • Veterinary care and rehabilitation
  • Adoption & fostering
  • Community education & outreach
  • Reporting channels for animal cruelty
